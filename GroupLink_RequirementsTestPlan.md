# GroupLink Requirements Testing
## Actors
- Provider P: Host
- Customer C: Member
- Service S: Group

### Use Cases
#### 1. Provider: Create provider profile (Sign up as a host):
1. Provider P1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.
2. P1 creates new groups S1 and S2 with values for searchable criteria C1, C2, C3 (C1=Name, C2=Type, C3=Location).  
P1 exits the app.

#### 2. Customer: Create customer profile (Sign up as a member:
1. Customer C1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.

#### 3. Customer:  View and apply to services:
1. Customer C2 logs in for the first time and creates a new profile.
2. C2 views available groups S1 and S2.
3. C2 applies to S1.

#### 4. Provider:  View and accept to application:
1. P1 views and accept C2's group application 

#### 4. Customer: Write review
1. C2 log in and views their groups.
2. C2 writes a positive review of service S1. C2 exits.

#### 5. Customer: Modify profile
1. C1 logs in and modifies their profile.
2. C1 views group S1 and the positive review.
3. C1 applies to S1. C1 exits.

#### 6. Provider:  Reply to Review, & Modify Profile use cases
1. Host P1 logs in and reads their review and replies with thanks. 
2. P1 modifies his profile. P1 exits.
